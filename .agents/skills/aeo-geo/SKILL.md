# 🧠 The Master AEO/GEO/AGO Skill: Technical & Tactical Playbook

This skill is the definitive knowledge base for dominating AI and Generative Search (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, Claude, and Google AI Overviews).

## 💎 The New Hierarchy of Search
1. **SEO**: Aims for **Clicks** (Traditional Ranking).
2. **GEO**: Aims for **Citations** (Reference links in AI responses).
3. **AGO (AI Generation Optimization)**: Aims for **Mentions** (Being described as the solution directly in the AI's "voice").

## 🛠 High-Impact Technical Implementation

### 1. Atomic Answer Blocks (AABs)
AI "cherry-picks" paragraphs. Structure every section as a stand-alone "Atomic Block":
- **Header**: Conversational Question (H2/H3).
- **TLDR Box**: A 2-3 sentence direct answer boxed in a `<div>` or specific class.
- **Verified Data**: Include 1 specific stat/metric (e.g., *"After analyzing 500+ campaigns..."*).
- **Conclusion**: A "Value Proposition" sentence.

### 2. The AI-First HTML Stack
- **Robots.txt**: Explicitly list `GPTBot`, `Claude-Bot`, `CCBot`, `Google-Extended`.
- **Bing Indexing**: Ensure 100% indexing in Bing Webmaster Tools (ChatGPT's primary live data source).
- **Structured Data (LLM-Ready)**:
    - Use JSON-LD for **Entity Recognition**.
    - Tag `FAQPage`, `Product`, `HowTo`, and `Review` schemas.
    - Highlight `author` and `datePublished` to signal "Content Freshness."
- **Transcripts**: If you have video/audio, you MUST provide a full text transcript on the same page. AI cannot "see" your video without it.

## 🌐 Brand Authority & Narrative Induction

### The "Trust Ecosystem"
AI uses third-party sites as its "Reference Library." You must be present where AI looks:
- **The Reddit Protocol**: Sort subreddits by "Hot" or "Rising." Answer threads with high upvotes. AI favors the most authoritative (upvoted) community voices.
- **YouTube Training**: In your YouTube settings, **allow AI models to train on your transcripts**. This is the highest ROI click for 2025/2026.
- **Citations vs Backlinks**: Brand mentions on Reddit and Quora correlate more strongly with AI visibility than traditional backlinks.

### Narrative Induction (Seeding the Model)
- **Guided Inquiry**: Seed the model by asking it about your industry and subtly providing your research as the "correction" to its gaps.
- **Category Creation**: Create and name your own category (e.g., "Inbound Marketing"). If the LLM associates you as the founder of a concept, you own the "Branded Intent."

## 📈 Strategic Workflow

### 1. Prompt Fanning (Query Fan-out)
AI doesn't search for "Email Marketing." It searches for sub-queries:
- *"Best email tool for solo creators?"*
- *"Cheapest email automation with high deliverability?"*
- *"Review of Kit vs Mailchimp 2026?"*
**Tactical**: Optimize your pages for these **predictable fan-out patterns**.

### 2. AI Visibility Priority Matrix
- **High Intent + Low Visibility**: Immediate content update priority.
- **High Traffic + High Visibility**: Maintenance & Freshness updates.
- **Low Traffic + High Visibility**: Analyze the intent and adapt the CTA.

### 3. Competitor Shadowing
- Use **Profound**, **PKI (Peak AI)**, or **F5Bot** to monitor when competitors are mentioned.
- Audit why the AI recommends them: Is it because of their **Pricing Page**, **Capterra reviews**, or a **Reddit thread**? Attack that specific source.
